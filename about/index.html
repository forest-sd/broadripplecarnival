About Broad Ripple Carnival

The Broad Ripple Carnival is an annual celebration that brings the Indianapolis community together for days of fun, food, and festivities. Located in the vibrant Broad Ripple Village neighborhood, our carnival has been a cherished tradition for families and friends for years.

What to Expect

From thrilling rides and classic carnival games to delicious food vendors and live entertainment, there's something for everyone at the Broad Ripple Carnival. Our event features local artisans, community organizations, and family-friendly activities that make it the highlight of the summer season.
